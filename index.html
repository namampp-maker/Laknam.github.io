About Laknam

Laknam Lakngoenchai is a Business Development & FMCG specialist based in Bangkok, Thailand. Currently completing a degree in Intercultural & Language Studies with a German concentration at Mahidol University International College, with a minor in International Relations & Global Affairs.

With hands-on experience at a haircare startup and a maritime electronics company, Laknam specialises in turning consumer data into strategic decisions — from designing 800-respondent research studies to building international distributor pipelines across Southeast Asia.

An exchange year at Eberhard Karls Universität Tübingen opened doors to representing Thailand at international forums — including an official delegation to the United Nations and NATO headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

Targeting a Management Trainee role in FMCG or beauty, where commercial rigour and cross-cultural range can drive real brand growth.
